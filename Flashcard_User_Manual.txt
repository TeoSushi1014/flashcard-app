================================================================
          HUONG DAN SU DUNG PHAN MEM FLASHCARD (C++)
================================================================

1. GIOI THIEU CHUNG
-------------------
Phan mem Flashcard giup nguoi dung on tap tu vung hoac kien thuc
thong qua co che the hai mat (Mat truoc/Mat sau). Du lieu duoc
luu tru va cap nhat truc tiep vao file van ban.

2. YEU CAU HE THONG & CAI DAT
-----------------------------
- He dieu hanh: Windows (Do phan mem su dung thu vien windows.h).
- Cau truc thu muc: De chuong trinh chay dung, cac file sau phai
  nam chung trong mot thu muc:
    + File chay (.exe) cua chuong trinh.
    + File du lieu: data.txt

3. CAU TRUC FILE DU LIEU (data.txt)
-----------------------------------
De them hoac sua noi dung the hoc, ban can mo file "data.txt" bang
Notepad va nhap theo quy tac sau:

    Mat_truoc;Mat_sau

Quy tac quan trong:
- Dung dau cham phay (;) de ngan cach giua cau hoi va cau tra loi.
- Moi dong tuong ung voi mot the.
- Khong de khoang trang thua o dong cuoi cung.

Vi du:
    Hello;Xin chao
    Computer;May tinh
    Software;Phan mem

4. CAC PHIM CHUC NANG
---------------------
Khi chuong trinh dang chay, su dung cac phim sau de dieu khien
(co the nhap chu hoa hoac chu thuong):

[ F ] - Lat the (Flip)
      Chuyen doi hien thi giua "Mat truoc" va "Mat sau" cua the
      hien tai.

[ N ] - Tiep theo (Next)
      Chuyen sang the ke tiep. Neu dang o the cuoi cung, chuong
      trinh se thong bao.

[ P ] - Quay lai (Previous)
      Quay lai the truoc do. Neu dang o the dau tien, chuong
      trinh se thong bao.

[ D ] - Xoa the (Delete)
      Xoa the dang hien thi khoi danh sach.
      LUU Y: Chuong trinh se tu dong cap nhat va LUU DE len file
      data.txt ngay lap tuc. Du lieu da xoa khong the khoi phuc.

[ Q ] - Thoat (Quit)
      Dong chuong trinh.
